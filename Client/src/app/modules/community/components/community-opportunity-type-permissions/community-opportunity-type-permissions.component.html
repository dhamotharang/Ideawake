• Ownership, Team Member, and Co-Submitter Settings

Enable idea ownership if you would like to be able to assign and hold individuals accountable for progression of ideas in your system.

If enabled, the idea submitter will be added automatically as the owner of the idea they submit.

Enable idea teams if you would like to encourage collaboration around ideas.

If enabled, idea submitters will be able to invite other collaborators to build a draft idea submission and co-submit an idea with them.

If enabled, the idea submitter will be added automatically as a team member of the idea they submit.

If enabled, child idea team members will automatically be added as team members to a parent idea.

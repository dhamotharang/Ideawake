Idea Ownership Settings

Enable idea ownership if you would like to be able to assign and hold individuals accountable for progression of ideas in your system.

If enabled, multiple idea owners will be able to be assigned to a single idea.

If enabled, the idea submitter will be added automatically as the owner of the idea they submit.

If enabled, owners will not be assigned as the owner to an idea until they confirm that they are the correct owner for it.

If enabled, only specific users that you select within your community will be able to be assigned as owners of an idea.

Describe Your Idea and the Problem it Solves

The first step to validating your idea is brainstorming the different problems that it helps solve. In order for customers to be willing to use our idea, it has to solve a problem that’s significant enough to the customer for them to get out their wallets and pay you.

It’s best to document as many potential problems as possible to work with, so think outside the box and collaborate with your team to brainstorm all the possibilities!
